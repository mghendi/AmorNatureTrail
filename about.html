About Us

Who are we?

Amor Nature Trail Limited is a Registered Tours and Travel private Company that specializes in outbound and inbound tourism in Kenya and abroad with offices in Nairobi and Narok. We are bonded and licensed by the Kenya Tourism Regulatory Authority (TRA).​ We are based in Kenya with offices approximately 5 kilometers from the Capital City of Nairobi, located at Nextgen Mall, Mombasa road with easy proximity to Wilson and Jomo Kenyatta International Airports. We are highly motivated, energetic professionals with practical knowledge to provide the best leisure travel solutions for our esteemed customers. Our expansion plans includes network of travel professionals to grow with us and set new trends with international and local travel industry.

Safari Packages, Team Building and Corporate Events.

Amor Nature Trail has also specialized in amazing safari trips that have greatly increased enthusiasm with nature among our clients.​ Bonding through games, bush meals and picnic dates have strengthened the relationships between the clients and the company.​
